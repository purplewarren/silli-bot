Privacy at Silli

Silli is designed privacy-first. The PWA processes audio on-device. No raw audio or video is uploaded. We only derive and store metrics (levels, stationarity, VAD fraction, etc.).

  • Raw media: discarded by default.
  • Derived data: stored in your bot log (events.jsonl & session summaries).
  • Transport: HTTPS end-to-end.
  • Auth: short-lived tokens for session ingest.
